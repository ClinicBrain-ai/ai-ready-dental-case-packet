# Dataset v0.2.7 Safeguards Checklist

Use this checklist before any abstraction of `CAND-010` or `CAND-016`.

This checklist is not an abstraction form and should not include case narrative.

## Scope Controls

- [ ] Only `CAND-010` and `CAND-016` are in scope.
- [ ] The remaining candidates remain on hold.
- [ ] No full-batch abstraction is authorized.
- [ ] Research Note 002 remains prohibited.

## Source-Distance Controls

- [ ] Do not copy source case text.
- [ ] Do not quote published case reports.
- [ ] Do not reproduce figures, tables, captions, or image descriptions.
- [ ] Do not reproduce distinctive clinical chronology.
- [ ] Use research-team wording only.

## Privacy and Identifiability Controls

- [ ] Do not include names, initials, institutions, locations, dates, record numbers, or exact timelines.
- [ ] Omit unnecessary demographic combinations.
- [ ] Generalize case-specific measurements and device details.
- [ ] Remove details that are not needed to test cognition reconstruction.

## Non-Guidance Controls

- [ ] Label all outputs as educational methodology artifacts.
- [ ] Avoid prescriptive clinical language.
- [ ] Do not imply patient-care recommendations.
- [ ] Do not describe the abstraction as clinical validation.
- [ ] Do not create real clinical documentation.
