# Source Eligibility Criteria

These criteria define whether a public educational case can be considered for Dataset v0.2 abstraction.

## Inclusion Criteria

- Publicly accessible educational case material.
- Clear initial presentation or referral-like artifact.
- Clear downstream assessment, specialist interpretation, or final plan.
- Enough information to infer cognition transformation across an artifact transition.
- No identifiable patient information.
- Suitable for educational or methodological abstraction.
- Citation or source metadata can be recorded without copying full source text.

## Exclusion Criteria

- Paywalled-only materials.
- Materials where full-text copying would be required to make the case usable.
- Identifiable patient details.
- Cases requiring clinical advice interpretation by the Dataset v0.2 team.
- Cases without enough artifact transition structure.
- Cases where copyright restrictions make reuse unsafe.
- Cases whose educational value depends on proprietary images, videos, or tables that cannot be abstracted safely.

## Eligibility Decision

Each candidate source should be classified as:

- eligible for abstraction;
- possibly eligible with limited paraphrase;
- not eligible;
- needs copyright or ethics review before use.

When uncertain, exclude the source from Dataset v0.2 and record the reason in the case index.
