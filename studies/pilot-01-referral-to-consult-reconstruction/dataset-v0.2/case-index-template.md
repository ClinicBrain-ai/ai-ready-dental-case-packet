## Risk Labels

Privacy risk:

- low: no identifiable patient information visible;
- moderate: age, rare condition, location, or timeline may require careful abstraction;
- high: identifiable details present; exclude unless formal review permits use.

Copyright risk:

- low: source allows educational reuse or only citation-level metadata will be stored;
- moderate: source can be cited but must be paraphrased carefully;
- high: source requires copying protected text or images; exclude.

## Notes

The case index is not the dataset itself. It is a screening and traceability artifact for deciding which sources can be safely abstracted.
