# Human Review Handoff

## 1. Purpose

This handoff supports human review of the Dataset v0.2.2 two-case abstraction dry run. The purpose is to decide whether `ABS-001` and `ABS-002` are safe to proceed to reconstruction coding.

This is not coding, not clinical validation, and not expansion to the remaining Dataset v0.2.1 candidates.

## 2. What the Reviewer Should Review

Review the two draft educational abstractions and the supporting safety materials:

- [ABS-001](../case-abstractions/ABS-001.md)
- [ABS-002](../case-abstractions/ABS-002.md)
- [Pre-push abstraction safety review](../pre-push-abstraction-safety-review.md)
- [Reviewer instructions](reviewer-instructions.md)
- [Source-distance checklist](source-distance-checklist.md)
- [Privacy and identifiability checklist](privacy-identifiability-checklist.md)
- [Non-guidance checklist](non-guidance-checklist.md)
- [Methodological usefulness checklist](methodological-usefulness-checklist.md)

- [ABS-002 human review form](ABS-002-human-review-form.md)
- [Coding gate decision](coding-gate-decision.md)

## 3. What the Reviewer Must Not Do

- Do not copy source case text into review files.
- Do not quote published case reports.
- Do not add identifiable details.
- Do not make clinical recommendations.
- Do not revise the abstractions into clinical documentation.
- Do not perform reconstruction coding.
- Do not mark the remaining 8 candidates ready for abstraction.

## 4. Review Sequence

1. Source-distance review.
2. Privacy / identifiability review.
3. Copyright / narrative proximity review.
4. Non-guidance review.
5. Methodological usefulness review.
6. Final coding gate decision.

## 5. Expected Output

The reviewer should complete:

- [ABS-002 human review form](ABS-002-human-review-form.md)
