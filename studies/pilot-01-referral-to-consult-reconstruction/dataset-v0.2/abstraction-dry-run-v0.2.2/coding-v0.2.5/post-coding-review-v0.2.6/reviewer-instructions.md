# Reviewer Instructions

## Review Scope

Review the Dataset v0.2.5 limited dry-run coding outputs for `ABS-001` and `ABS-002` only.

This review evaluates method safety and usefulness. It does not validate clinical content and does not authorize clinical use.

## Materials to Use

Use only repository materials in the v0.2.5 coding folder:

- `../case-coding/ABS-001-coding.md`
- `../case-coding/ABS-002-coding.md`
- `../results-table.md`
- `../transformation-summary.md`
- `../coding-memo.md`
- `../limited-boundaries.md`
- `../post-coding-review-checklist.md`
- `../post-coding-review-gate.md`

## Materials Not to Use

Do not use:

- source case reports;
- copied source text;
- external clinical references for interpreting care decisions;
- any additional candidate abstractions.

## Review Criteria

Assess whether:

- coding stayed within the two approved abstractions;
- no source-text reuse or patient-identifying information was introduced;
- no clinical guidance was created;
- reconstruction claims are appropriately cautious;
- coding categories are supported by the abstractions;
- rubric scores are interpretable;
- confidence labels are justified;
- uncertainty and missing evidence are handled clearly;
- the next step should be second-coder repeat, revision, limited expansion, or pause.

## Decision Discipline

Leave the post-coding gate closed unless the completed decision record explicitly authorizes a next step.

If uncertain, prefer a second-coder repeat of `ABS-001` and `ABS-002` before expanding to more cases.
